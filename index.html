House & Land Packages

What is a Home and Land Package?

A house package made to fit a specific block of land. Split contracts are used (one for land, one for construction). Packages come as either "fixed" or "flexible".

What is a Flexible House & Land Package?

Flexible packages allow you to adjust features to your taste while staying within your budget. Work with our specialists to customize floor plans or upgrades.
