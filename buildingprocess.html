Our Building Process

Simplicity is Key!

With years of expertise in constructing new homes across Sydney, NSW, we’ve refined a simple and effective 6-stages house-building process. Build your dream home with confidence, the easy way.

Stage 1: Home Selection and Tender

Every dream home begins with a conversation. Schedule a consultation with our home design specialist to find the perfect design tailored to you and your family’s lifestyle. Once we receive your Tender fee, we arrange a site inspection of your land and order preliminary planning documents along with other essential property information.

Stage 2: Fixed Price Tender

We prepare a comprehensive Tender, which your Home Sales Consultant will present and explain in detail. Once you have accepted the Tender and paid the Tender Acceptance fee, we will move forward with preparing your architectural and engineering plans.

Stage 3: Final Plans, Fittings Fixtures and Equipment Schedule (FF & E)

This is where the exciting part begins. With the support of our interior design team, you’ll refine your design and select from a carefully curated range of finishes, fixtures, and details.

Stage 4: Approvals & Paperwork

Building approval timelines can differ, as each council or certifier has their own set of requirements. We take great care to prepare plans that meet these guidelines; however, all approvals are ultimately subject to the decision of the relevant authority. Once approval is granted by Council, CDC, or certifier, the necessary documents and reports will be arranged to obtain the Construction Certificate so your project can move forward smoothly.

Stage 5: Construction

Your nominated site supervisor will keep you informed through regular communication and detailed weekly progress reports. Every aspect of the build is managed with the same care and attention as if we were constructing our own home.

Stage 6: Handover

Completion day marks the successful culmination of your project. Upon receiving your keys, you will be guided through a comprehensive walkthrough, ensuring familiarity with all features and finishes. This process signifies the official handover and the readiness of your home for occupancy.
